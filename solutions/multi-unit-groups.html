For Multi-unit groups

Every location, one source of truth

Running a group means asking the same questions of every venue — and getting answers in five different formats. Mise gives multi-unit operators one system across all locations: portfolio rollups, per-venue P&L, shared menus and recipes, and access control that scales.

What matters most here

The modules a multi-unit group operator leans on

Portfolio & per-location P&L

See the group at a glance, then drill into any single venue’s live prime-cost P&L — same numbers, same basis, everywhere. Financials →

Centralized menus & recipes

Define a recipe or a menu once and roll it out across venues, with local price overrides where you need them.

Cross-location reporting

Compare sales, labor % and food cost across locations on one screen — spot the outlier without exporting five spreadsheets.

Role-based access that scales

Above-store managers, GMs and staff each get exactly the right view, enforced server-side. Security →

Operator console

A support-and-operations console for the people who run the group, not just a single restaurant.

Consistent guest data

One guest CRM across the group, so a VIP at one location is recognized at the next. Guest CRM →

One source of truth

Replaces per-location POS silos, a separate accounting rollup and a pile of reconciled spreadsheets.

Because it’s all one system, a closed check moves sales, inventory, labor and the P&L in the same second — no exports, no re-keying, no spreadsheet that’s always a day behind.
