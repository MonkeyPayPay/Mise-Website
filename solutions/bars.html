The modules a bar operator leans on

Fast tabs & quick-fire POS

Open tabs, pre-auth cards, split and transfer fast — the POS keeps up with a slammed bar and every ring lands in the same system.

Pour cost & variance

Recipe-cost every cocktail, then compare theoretical vs. actual usage to catch over-pours and shrink instead of eating them. Inventory →

Peak-night labor

Schedule for the rush and watch labor % against live sales, with overtime flagged before it’s worked. Scheduling →

Comp & void controls

Comps, voids and discounts are role-gated and recorded with who and when — so generosity doesn’t quietly become leakage.

Auto-86 the run-outs

When a bottle or keg hits zero, the menu updates and the team is notified in the same second.
