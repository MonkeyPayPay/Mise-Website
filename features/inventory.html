What's inside

From the shelf to the plate cost

Live depletion from POS

Recipe costing draws every ingredient down as checks close — no end-of-week guessing about what you actually used.

Moving-average costing

On-hand is valued at a moving-average cost that updates with every receipt, so plate cost and inventory value are always real.

Recipe & plate costing

Cost every dish from its ingredients, see food-cost %, and price with confidence. Sub-recipes and prep items included.

Waste log & variance

Log waste, run physical counts, and see variance (theoretical vs. actual) so shrink and over-pours surface instead of hiding.

Auto-86 at zero

When an ingredient runs dry, its menu items flip to 86 automatically and the #kitchen channel is notified.

Par-based prep plans

Suggested prep and order quantities from live stock against par, so the line is set and nothing runs out mid-service.

Menu engineering

Know your stars from your dogs

Because Mise has both the margin and the sales counts, every dish lands on the menu-engineering quadrant automatically — popular & profitable (stars) through neither (dogs).

  • Stars, plowhorses, puzzles and dogs, from real data
  • Contribution margin per dish, not just food-cost %
  • Re-cost the whole menu the instant a vendor price moves
