Why teams switch

The guest profile and the check are the same system

Spend that's never a day behind

SevenRooms enriches profiles with spend through a POS integration. In Mise the check is the record, so lifetime spend and visit counts update the second a check closes.

No per-cover economics

You pay per location, not per guest who walks in. The reservation book doesn't get more expensive on your busiest nights.

The floor stays in sync

Reservations, the live floor plan and the POS share one table status — a paid check frees the table automatically, no double entry at the host stand.

Guests and operations, one bill

VIP recognition and reservation notes run on the same system as inventory, labor and the P&L — one product to buy, learn and support.

An honest take

Which one is right for you

When SevenRooms fits

SevenRooms is a great fit for groups that want a marketing-forward, standalone guest-experience and CRM platform with deep automation, and are willing to integrate it with their existing POS and pay its cover-based pricing.

When Mise fits

Mise fits when you want the reservation book, the waitlist and the guest CRM to be part of the same system that runs the floor and the check — with spend that's always current and no per-cover fees.
