Connects to the tools you already run

Mise is one system by design — but it meets your restaurant where it is. Keep your POS, your identity provider and your accountant; Mise plugs into payments, messaging, identity and your books, and can even run on top of the POS you already have.

Stripe

Payments

Card processing & tips

Take cards on the same terminal that rings the check; tips, refunds and adjustments flow straight into the P&L.

Deposits & card-on-file

Hold event deposits or keep a card on file for private dining — the guest enters it securely and Mise stores only the token, never the PAN.

Twilio & SendGrid

Messaging

SMS confirmations & waitlist

Reservation confirmations, waitlist “your table’s ready” texts and reminders go out over Twilio.

Transactional email

Booking confirmations, receipts and event proposals send over SendGrid with your restaurant’s branding.

OIDC SSO & MFA

Identity & access

Single sign-on

Connect your identity provider over OpenID Connect so staff log in with the accounts you already manage.

Multi-factor auth

TOTP-based MFA for manager and operator accounts, enforced by role.

GL export, journals & warehouse

Accounting & data

GL-categorized export

Every invoice and daily sales summary exports as CSV, mapped to your GL categories — drop it straight into QuickBooks or Xero.

Journal push & warehouse feed

Optional GL journal push to your accounting middleware, plus a data-warehouse feed on Enterprise for BI tools.

Toast, Square & Lightspeed

POS ingestion

Run on your current POS

Webhook adapters turn a closed check on Toast, Square or Lightspeed into one normalized event that fans out to inventory, financials and the guest CRM.

Or use the Mise POS

Prefer one system end to end? The built-in Mise POS and KDS emit the same events natively — no adapter required.

OpenTable, Resy & Google

Reservations reach

Guest Center deep-links

The Guest Center helps diners book any restaurant — Mise-native bookings land on the host stand instantly; everything else deep-links out to OpenTable, Resy or Google.

Embeddable booking widget

Drop the Mise booking widget on your own site — no per-cover fees, no separate login.
