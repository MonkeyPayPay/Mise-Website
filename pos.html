Why it's different

Built for the floor, wired to everything

Tablet-first

Coursing, modifiers, seat & split by tap. Fire, hold and transfer without hunting through menus. Handhelds and stationary terminals share one ticket.

Offline-resilient

Internet blips mid-rush? Keep ringing. Checks queue locally and reconcile the moment you reconnect — no lost sales, no frozen terminal.

Real-time depletion

Every item is recipe-costed, so a sale draws down inventory live and auto-86's a dish the second an ingredient hits zero.

Guest-aware

The check knows the guest — allergies flagged, VIPs surfaced, visit count and lifetime spend updated automatically when it closes.

Your payments, your choice

First-party card processing built in, or keep your processor. Tips, surcharges and split tenders handled cleanly.

Or bring your POS

Already on Toast, Square or Lightspeed? Webhook adapters turn their closed checks into the same normalized event — adopt Mise without ripping out the register.

The one-event model

Close a check. The restaurant updates itself.

A single closed check adds to today's flash and live P&L, depletes inventory through recipe costing, posts any 86 to #kitchen, and grows the guest's profile — in the same second, with no export and no re-keying.
