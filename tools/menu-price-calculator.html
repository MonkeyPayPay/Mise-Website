The formula

Menu price = Plate cost ÷ Target food-cost %

Divide a dish’s plate cost by your target food-cost percentage (as a decimal) to get the price that hits that target. A $4.50 plate at a 30% target prices to $15.00, keeping $10.50 toward labor, overhead and profit. Menu-price math is only as good as your plate cost — and plate cost moves every time a vendor price does. Mise re-costs your whole menu the instant a price changes, and shows you which dishes are stars and which are dogs.
