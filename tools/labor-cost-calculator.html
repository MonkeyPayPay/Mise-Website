The formula

Labor cost % = Total labor ÷ Total sales

Add wages and salaries to payroll taxes and benefits for your fully-loaded labor cost, then divide by sales. Full-service restaurants commonly run labor around 30–35% of sales, but it swings by service model. Mise measures labor against live sales as checks close and flags overtime before it’s worked — so the number is something you manage during the week, not discover on the payroll report.
