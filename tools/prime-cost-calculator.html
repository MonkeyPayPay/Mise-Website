The formula

Prime cost = COGS + total labor

Add your cost of goods sold (all the food and beverage you used) to your total labor cost (wages, payroll taxes and benefits). Divide by sales for prime-cost % . Most full-service operators aim to keep it at or below 60% — because everything else (rent, utilities, insurance, profit) has to come out of what’s left.
